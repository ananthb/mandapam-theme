## About Our Venue

Sri Lakshmi Mandapam has been hosting memorable events for over two decades. Located in the heart of the city, our venue combines traditional architecture with modern amenities.

### Why Choose Us?

- **Prime Location**: Easily accessible from all parts of the city
- **Spacious Halls**: Can accommodate events of all sizes
- **Modern Amenities**: Air conditioning, sound system, and lighting
- **Experienced Staff**: Dedicated team to assist with all arrangements
- **Flexible Packages**: Customizable options to suit your needs
